About Empowering the Nation

About us

Inspired by her own family's lack of training opportunities, Precious Radebe founded Empowering the Nation to help others. Our school provides domestic workers and gardeners with the professional skills needed to build a better future and achieve their full potential.

Our Mission:

Helping Millions of people grow Better

Our mission is to empower domestic workers with high-quality vocational training. We provide marketable skills that leads to higher wages and new business opportunities, building a stronger community one person at a time.

Established in 2022 in Johannesburg. Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained through our 6-month and 6-week programs.
